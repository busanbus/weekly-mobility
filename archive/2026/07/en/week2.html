Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, and Transport

Delegation of Authority to the Metropolitan Planning Commission for Urban Rail Projects, from Planning to Construction

The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, and Transport has centralized authority—from the planning stage, such as the approval of urban rail network construction plans, through to follow-up projects—under the Metropolitan Area Transportation Commission, enabling it to handle deliberations and approvals in a continuous manner.

Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, and Transport

Guidelines Established for Safe Operation of Driverless Autonomous Vehicles

The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport has established guidelines setting out specific safety requirements—including a minimum driving record of 15,000 km, remote monitoring, emergency stop capabilities, and system redundancy—to allow Level 4 autonomous vehicles to undergo field testing after obtaining a temporary operating permit.
