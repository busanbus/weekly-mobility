Department of the Interior

Selected as a participant in the Gwangju Autonomous Driving Pilot City Project

MOLIT selected Hyundai Motor Company in the vehicle and platform sector and Samsung Fire & Marine in the insurance sector as participants in the K-Autonomous Driving Cooperation Model promoted by Gwangju Metropolitan City, the first autonomous driving pilot city in Korea, to establish an integrated support system for the supply of demonstration vehicles, transportation platforms, and dedicated insurance.

Seoul

Seoul-type big data standard analysis model, public facility optimal location analysis model service

The Seoul Metropolitan Government operates an online service that automatically recommends candidate locations for five types of facilities, including CCTV, electric vehicle charging stations, and smart shelters, by adding an optimal location model for public facilities to the 'Seoul-type big data standard analysis model' that standardizes location analysis scattered by city and district.

Pods

Unveils Ford Pro AI, a platform dedicated to commercial fleet operations

Ford unveiled Ford Pro AI for commercial fleet operators, where AI analyzes vehicle data (speed, fuel, engine status, seat belts, etc.) in real-time to provide operational insights such as timing maintenance, optimizing fuel efficiency, and improving driving patterns, and AI chatbots to answer questions and delegate automated tasks.
