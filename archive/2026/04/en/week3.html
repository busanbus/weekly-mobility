First autonomous paid freight on highways

The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, and Transport has licensed Rideflux for autonomous paid cargo transportation for the first time in Korea, and from June, the company will start delivering courier services on the Seoul-Jincheon Jungbu Expressway.

1 month exemption for local buses and late-night freight vehicles from tolls on the Revenue Highway

The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, and Transport has decided to exempt local buses and late-night freight vehicles from paying tolls on the National Revenue Highway for a month from April 16 to May 16 to ease the burden on the transportation industry due to high gas prices.
