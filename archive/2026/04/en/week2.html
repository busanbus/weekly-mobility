Department of the Interior

Promoting liability standards and compensation for autonomous vehicle accidents

MOLIT has formed an 'Accident Liability Task Force' to clarify the structure of responsibility sharing and standardize compensation procedures in the event of an autonomous vehicle accident, and is supporting the establishment of guidelines and revision of related laws.

50% off the Climate Allies Card to encourage public transportation use during high gas prices

In response to the high oil prices, the city of Seoul is offering a 30,000 won monthly payback to users of the 30-day Climate Companion Card from April to June.

Busan

Notification of the Basic Plan for Urban Maintenance for Aging Planning

The city of Busan has notified the Basic Plan for the Maintenance of Old Planned Cities (Haeundae, Hwaseong, Geumgok, Dadae, Mandeok, Mora, Gaegeum/Danggam), which are six old planned cities (Haeundae, Hwaseong/Geumgok, Dadae, Mandeok, Mora, and Gaegeum/Danggam) that have been developed for more than 20 years, with the goal of transforming the residential environment and transforming Busan-type future cities.
