Competition for Seats on Regional Buses During Commute to New Towns in Incheon and Gyeonggi Intensifies

There has been an increase in the number of commuters traveling from the greater Seoul area to Seoul who, in an effort to secure seats on express buses, are turning back to the terminal or the previous bus stop, or are taking city buses or taxis heading in the opposite direction first.

Kia Motors Exits the Bus Manufacturing Business

Faced with a decline in business viability due to an onslaught of low-cost electric buses from China and stricter global emissions regulations, Kia has officially announced the discontinuation of production of its “Grandbird” large bus and decided to withdraw from the bus business for the first time in about 60 years.
