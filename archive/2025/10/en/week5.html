MOLIT

Revision of Low-Floor Bus Standard Model Criteria

The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport is revising the standard model criteria for low-floor buses to expand their adoption to include city seat buses and establish new standards for convenience and safety equipment such as wheelchair restraint systems (safety belts).

Demonstration of AI-Based Predictive Pedestrian Safety Service

Cheonan City is conducting a demonstration operation of an AI-based predictive pedestrian safety service at four major intersections starting in August 2025, in collaboration with the Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI). This marks the first such initiative in Korea.
