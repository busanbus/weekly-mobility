Jeju

Nation's First Free Public Transportation for Youth

Starting in August, Jeju Island will implement a completely free bus fare policy for teenagers, the first of its kind in the nation. This policy is an important measure to expand youth public transportation usage and enhance youth traffic safety.

MOLIT

Expansion of Chartered Buses for School Commuting Through Contracts with Office of Education (Amendment to Passenger Transport Service Act)

Chartered buses for school commuting will be expanded nationwide through direct contracts with the Office of Education. The amendment to the Passenger Transport Service Act has made the operation of chartered buses for school commuting possible.
