T-money's Injunction Request Dismissed, Mybi Confirmed as Final Operator

With the court's dismissal of T-money's injunction request regarding the Busan transportation card project, Mybi has been confirmed as the final operator. Consequently, Mybi will continue to operate the public transportation payment system in the Busan area.
