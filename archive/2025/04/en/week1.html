Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport

Launch of the Autonomous Driving Regional Council

The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport has officially launched the 'Autonomous Vehicle Demonstration Zone Regional Council,' involving director-level officials from 17 cities and provinces nationwide. The government and local authorities will share policies to promote the commercialization of autonomous vehicles and ensure the systematic operation and management of demonstration zones.

Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport

Limited Private Opening of Transportation Card Raw Data

From April 1st, the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport and the Korea Transportation Safety Authority will provide limited access to transportation card big data to the private sector through a secure data safe zone. Private companies and research institutions will analyze various data, including public transport boarding and alighting times and usage patterns.

Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport

Explanatory Material on Role-Sharing Between Gadeok New Airport and Gimhae Airport Distributed

Regarding the role-sharing between Gadeok New Airport and Gimhae Airport after the new airport opens, it has been announced that specifics will be confirmed during the operational preparation stage before the opening.

Jeju

Nation's First Project to Utilize Electric Buses as Mobile Energy Storage Systems in Jeju

Jeju Special Self-Governing Province is promoting an innovative project to utilize electric buses as mobile energy storage systems for the first time in the nation. A business agreement has been signed for the commercialization of Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) technology.

Busan Transportation Corporation

Exhibition and Experience Center Using First Subway Train from 1985 to Open in July

From July, the Busan Transportation Corporation will open an exhibition and experience center to the public, utilizing the first subway train introduced during the opening of Busan Metro Line 1 in 1985. The event commemorates the 40th anniversary of the Busan Metro opening and is planned to be operated as a permanent exhibition hall in the future.
