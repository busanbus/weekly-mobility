Daejeon Transportation Corporation to Introduce 3-Car Electric Articulated Buses

Daejeon Transportation Corporation will introduce the nation's first three-car electric articulated buses for a pilot run at the end of the year. This new high-capacity mode of transport, capable of carrying up to 250 passengers at once, is expected to alleviate traffic congestion and innovate public transportation.
