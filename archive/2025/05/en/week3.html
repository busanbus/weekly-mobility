KORAIL

Popularity of Foreigner-Exclusive Transportation + Communication Pass

A foreigner-exclusive pass (Namane Card), a collaboration between KORAIL and LG Uplus, is gaining attention. It offers unlimited use of subways, buses, and data networks nationwide for 3 to 5 days, significantly improving convenience for tourists.

Seoul

Village Bus Association Announces Withdrawal from Public Transit Free Transfer System

The Seoul Village Bus Association has resolved to withdraw from the public transportation free transfer system. They have stated that they will not hesitate to suspend operations if their demands for a fare increase and reflection of transfer losses are not met.

Gwangju

Promotional Character Competition

In celebration of the 'Visit Gwangju Year', a 'Bitdori' character wedding event was held outdoors at City Hall on the 24th. Characters from local governments across the country participated as guests, aiming to promote the region and vitalize the content industry simultaneously.

Gwangandaegyo Bridge Commuting Discount Hours Extended
