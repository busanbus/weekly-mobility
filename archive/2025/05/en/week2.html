Ulsan

Completion of the Nation's Largest Hydrogen Shipping Center

The nation's largest hydrogen shipping center, capable of producing 43 tons per day, has been completed in Ulsan. It is the first in the country to use domestically produced compressors, and it is expected to significantly expand the hydrogen infrastructure for public transportation.

Daejeon

Selected for Autonomous Driving V2X Demonstration Project

Daejeon City has been selected for the 'Heterogeneous V2X Cooperative Driving Communication Technology' demonstration project led by the Ministry of Science and ICT, and will now fully pursue advanced mobility demonstrations such as integrated control and remote operation of autonomous vehicles.

Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport

Announcement of Measures to Reduce Road Traffic Fatalities

The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport has announced comprehensive traffic safety measures, including shortening crosswalk signal times for pedestrian protection, expanding pedestrian-priority roads, strengthening penalties for drunk and dangerous driving, and establishing new safety standards for two-wheeled vehicles.

Jeju

Introduction of Fully Public Bus System Fails

The introduction of a fully public bus system in Jeju has been canceled after being rejected by the Policy Development Deliberation Committee. It was pointed out that various discussions are needed regarding interests, sustainability, and feasibility.
