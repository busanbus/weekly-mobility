National

Transit Pass Operation Status

Local governments, including the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport, Seoul, Gyeonggi, and Busan, are introducing various forms of transit passes. With large variations in user benefits, refund methods, and loss compensation among municipalities, policy adjustments are needed for sustainability.

Ulsan

Ulsan Expands Hydrogen Green Mobility Demonstration

Charging infrastructure, previously limited to hydrogen cars, is being expanded to all hydrogen mobility, including forklifts and ships. The scope of demonstration has been widened, and the introduction of mobile hydrogen charging stations is also underway.

Jeju

Operation of BRT Stabilization Task Force

Jeju Island has formed a BRT stabilization task force with the Road Traffic Authority, the autonomous police force, and private experts. They plan to comprehensively review facility improvements, resolve resident inconveniences, and consider future expansion plans.

Jeju

Nation's First BRT Island-Style Stop in Operation

The 'island-style stop' introduced by Jeju Island for the first time in the nation has opened on the BRT Seogwang-ro section. This allows users to access both inbound and outbound buses from a single stop, increasing transfer convenience and expecting to improve public transport punctuality and speed.

FTC

Kakao T Fined 3.882 Billion KRW for Unfair Fee Collection

The Fair Trade Commission has imposed a fine of 3.882 billion KRW and a corrective order on Kakao T for uniformly collecting 20% of the franchise taxi fare as a commission and charging fees for transactions where the app was not actually used. Kakao has announced it will file an administrative lawsuit, claiming its original intention was undermined.

International

Tesla Launches Robotaxi

Tesla has announced the launch of its robotaxi service based on fully autonomous driving technology. It aims to safely transport passengers to their destinations without a driver, heralding a paradigm shift in urban transportation.
