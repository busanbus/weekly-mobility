Jeju

BRT Implementation: Conflicting Views Between Jeju and its Residents

While Jeju Province announced a 43% increase in bus speeds during rush hour after the opening of the Seogwang-ro BRT section, residents point out that on-site confusion, including traffic congestion and inconvenience at island-style bus stops, persists.

Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance

Insurance Launched to Cover Taxi Fares for Subway Delays

Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance has launched the industry's first 'Subway Delay Insurance,' which compensates up to 30,000 KRW for alternative transportation costs, such as taxis, if the metropolitan subway is delayed by 30 minutes or more.

Jincheon & Eumseong, Chungbuk

Status of Nationwide Free Bus Project

As a result of making rural buses completely free since January this year in Jincheon and Eumseong counties, passenger numbers have increased by over 30% in six months compared to the previous year. The use by transportation-vulnerable groups like the elderly and youth has significantly increased, showing positive effects on the local economy and tourism.

International

EREV Market Expands to Address EV Chasm

Extended-Range Electric Vehicles (EREV), an intermediate step between EVs and hybrids, are growing at an average annual rate of 20% in the global market, with mass production accelerating, led by China. Hyundai plans to launch an EREV with a range of 900 km on a full charge by 2027.

Seoul

Expansion of Voucher Taxis for People with Disabilities

Seoul is expanding its voucher taxi service for people with severe walking disabilities by increasing the number of operating vehicles and service areas. This move aims to improve the mobility rights of people with disabilities and is expected to enhance their convenience and social participation.
