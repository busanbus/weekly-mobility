The city of Busan is introducing the nation's largest fleet of eco-friendly hydrogen-powered cleaning trucks. A total of 65 vehicles will be deployed in stages from this year to 2028, featuring zero emissions, low noise, and low vibration to improve waste collection and the urban environment.
