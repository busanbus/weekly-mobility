Busan Police Agency

Statistical evidence supporting the perception that driving in Busan is rough

The perception that driving in Busan is rough has been statistically proven. Over the past five years, the number of reports of reckless driving and retaliatory driving in Busan has been among the highest in the country, and more than 10,000 traffic accidents caused by traffic violations occur every year.

Industry

Domestic diesel passenger car model discontinuation

Domestic diesel passenger car models have entered the process of discontinuation due to declining demand and stricter environmental regulations. Hyundai Motor has stopped production of the Tucson and Staria diesel models in August 2025, and Kia has discontinued the diesel model in the 2026 model year.
