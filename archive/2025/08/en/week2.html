Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy

Call for proposals for designation of hydrogen-specialized complexes

The Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy has begun accepting applications for the designation of new hydrogen-specialized complexes from local governments nationwide. Local governments wishing to apply must submit the relevant documents, including the application form and development plan, by August 14.

Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy

Promoting mandatory installation of renewable energy (solar) equipment in public parking lots

All public parking lots established and operated by national and local governments and public institutions will be required to install renewable energy equipment such as solar power.
