Busan

Naeseong~Haewoon University BRT Section Designated as Autonomous Vehicle Pilot Zone

The Busan Naeseong~Haewoon University BRT section has been designated as an autonomous vehicle pilot zone. After a test in the second half of this year, from next year, three autonomous electric buses are expected to operate regularly during peak hours.

Seoul

Village Bus Replacement and Expansion

In Seongdong-gu, the 'Success Bus' is being introduced to maintain the village bus network, in Dongjak-gu, autonomous village bus pilot projects are underway, and in Jung-gu, shuttle buses for public facilities are being introduced to expand alternative transportation.

Autonomous Mobility

2025 Autonomous Mobility Industry Fair (COEX, 7/9~7/11)

The 2025 Autonomous Mobility Industry Fair (AME 2025) will be held at COEX, Seoul, from July 9 to 11. The event will showcase the latest technologies and solutions in the autonomous mobility industry, including autonomous vehicles, LiDAR, AI, and vehicle platforms.
